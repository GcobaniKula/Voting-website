ABOUT US

Gcobani Informatics was founded by Gcobani Kula in the year 2024. The company's aim is to lead and host people in the digital community, by proving new software technology that will create digital communities at a reasonable price. Mr Gcobani Kula created this website to allow people in rural communities and other communities that can not access the voting station to exercise their right to voting. The other aim of this website is to teach people about the history of election and political parties, this includes each party's history, current leadership,ideologies and menifestor.
